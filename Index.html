What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating systems or machines that can perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence. This includes the ability to learn from experience, recognize patterns, understand natural language, make decisions, and solve problems. AI can be used in a wide range of applications, from autonomous vehicles to virtual assistants, medical diagnosis, data analysis, and more.
